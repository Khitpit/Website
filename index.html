Chopstix

I embarked on an engaging project inspired by childhood memories of the 'Chopsticks' finger game. Finding no existing solutions, I independently applied linear algebra and graph traversal algorithms to formulate and solve challenges, resulting in an AI that consistently wins the game. This endeavor showcased my adeptness in both mathematical theory and practical application, while also potentially contributing to game theory, AI, and algorithmic domains. Sharing these findings with relevant communities could open avenues for further exploration and innovation.
