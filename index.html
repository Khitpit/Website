Chopstix

I embarked on an engaging project inspired by childhood memories of the 'Chopsticks' finger game. This endeavor showcased my adeptness in both mathematical theory and practical application, while also potentially contributing to game theory, AI, and algorithmic domains. Sharing these findings with relevant communities could open avenues for further exploration and innovation.
